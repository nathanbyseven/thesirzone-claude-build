The Sirzone Academic
Acceleration Program

Sirzone's academic acceleration program supports Grade 10–12 learners through a structured system of weekly mentorship, guided practice, personalised feedback, and direct support. It is designed to help learners build momentum through the school year and improve performance with consistency.

Structured Journey

The 4-Phase Journey

The program follows a structured yearly cycle that helps learners build consistency, close learning gaps, and prepare for final assessments with confidence. Learners who join mid-year can enter at the most relevant phase.

Phase 1
Foundation Phase
Jan – Feb
  • Diagnostic assessment
  • Goal setting
  • Weak-spot identification
Phase 2
Mastery Phase
Mar – Jun
  • Weekly skill-building sessions
  • Targeted practice
  • Concept reinforcement
Phase 3
Performance Phase
Jul – Sep
  • Test preparation
  • Progress reviews
  • Momentum building
Phase 4
Exam Acceleration Phase
Oct – Exams
  • Intensive revision drills
  • Mock exams
  • Final performance optimisation

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sirzone tutoring or mentorship?
Sirzone is a structured academic mentorship program designed to improve performance through weekly support, accountability, guided practice, and exam preparation.
Which grades do you support?
Sirzone supports Grade 10–12 learners in Mathematics and Physical Sciences.
Can learners join mid-year?
Yes. Learners can join during the year and enter the phase most relevant to their academic needs.
How are payments handled?
Payments are billed monthly in advance. Quarterly upfront payment options include a 10% discount. Payments are non-refundable.
What support happens outside sessions?
Learners receive ongoing WhatsApp guidance, task feedback, and accountability between sessions.
Are sessions online or in person?
All sessions are conducted online. In-person arrangements are for institutional programmes only.
